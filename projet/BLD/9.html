Marinated Vegetable Salad





25 Minutes
101 Calories
10 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-6 sliced green onions, tops included
-12 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
-2 cups mushrooms, quartered
-1/2 cup broccoli florets, cut in half
-1 cup red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
-2 tbsp olive oil
-2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
-2 tbsp lemon juice
-1 tsp salt-free Mrs. DashÂ® Lemon Pepper Seasoning Blend
-1 tsp basil

Directions

  1. In a large bowl combine the onions, tomatoes, mushrooms, broccoli, and pepper.
  2. In a small bowl combine the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, salt-free seasoning, and basil.
  3. Add the dressing to the vegetable mixture and mix well.
  4. Chill for 15 minutes before serving.
  5. Makes 4 servings.

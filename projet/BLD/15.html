Potato Salad





20 Minutes
105 Calories
11 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-2 cups red potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
-1 cup water
-1/4 cup diced celery
-1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
-1/4 cup diced red onion
-2 tbsp low-fat mayonnaise
-2 tbsp low-fat sour cream
-1 tbsp honey mustard
-1/2 tsp onion powder
-1/2 tsp basil
-1/2 tsp salt-free Mrs. DashÂ® Lemon Pepper Seasoning Blend

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, bring the potatoes and water to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to simmer. Cook for 5 to 6 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  2. Drain the water and set aside to let the potato pieces cool to room temperature.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large bowl combine the celery, pepper, and onion.
  4. In a small bowl combine and mix mayonnaise, sour cream, honey mustard, onion powder, basil, and salt-free seasoning.
  5. Add the cooked potatoes and the dressing to the celery mixture and mix well.
  6. Makes 4 servings.

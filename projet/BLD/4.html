Whole-grain Waffles





35 Minutes
261 Calories
9 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-1 1/2 cups dry oatmeal
-1/2 cup whole-wheat flour
-1/4 cup cornmeal
-2 tbsp brown sugar
-1/4 tsp salt
-1/4 cup dry instant nonfat milk
-1 1/2 tbsp canola oil
-2 1/4 cups warm water
-Nonstick cooking spray

Directions

  1. Place dry oatmeal in a blender and blend for 5 to 10 seconds to make oat flour. Pour into a large bowl and add flour, cornmeal, brown sugar, salt, and dry milk. Mix well.
  2. Add canola oil and warm water. Mix with a wire whisk and let stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Spray a 7-inch round or small waffle iron with nonstick cooking spray. When hot, pour 3/4 cup of batter on the waffle iron and cook 3 to 4 minutes or until golden brown. Repeat to make 4 waffles.
  4. Makes 4 servings

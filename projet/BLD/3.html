Mixed Fruit Salad





5 Minutes
104 Calories
6 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-1 chopped apple
-1 orange, cut into pieces
-18 seedless grapes
-1 banana, sliced
-1/2 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
-2 tsp honey

Directions

  1. In a large bowl combine the apple, orange, grapes, and banana.
  2. In a small bowl combine and mix the yogurt and honey.
  3. dd the yogurt dressing to the fruit mixture and mix well.
  4. Makes 4 servings.

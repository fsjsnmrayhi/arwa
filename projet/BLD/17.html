Seasoned Lentils Over Potatoes





60 Minutes
489 Calories
15 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-4 (10-oz) large russet potatoes, (washed and dried)
-Nonstick cooking spray
-1 tbsp olive oil
-1/2 cup chopped onion
-4 minced garlic cloves
-1/2 cup chopped celery
-2 cups sliced mushrooms
-3 cups water
-3/4 cup dry lentils
-1/2 (6-oz) can tomato paste, no salt added
-1 cup sliced carrots
-1 tsp cumin
-1 tsp curry powder
-1 tsp onion powder
-1 tsp salt

Directions

    Fat 3g
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Pierce potatoes in several places to allow steam to escape. Place potatoes on oven rack or baking sheet. Bake 40 to 55 minutes or until tender when pierced with a fork. (Or microwave potatoes on high for 5 to 9 minutes each.)
  3. Meanwhile, spray a large saucepan with nonstick cooking spray. Place saucepan over medium-high heat and add olive oil. When hot, add onion, garlic, and celery and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add the mushrooms, water, lentils, tomato paste, carrots, cumin, curry powder, onion powder, and salt.
  5. Bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to simmer. Cook for 40 to 45 minutes or until lentils are tender.
  6. To serve, cut the baked potatoes in half and top with the seasoned lentil mixture.
  7. Makes 4 servings.

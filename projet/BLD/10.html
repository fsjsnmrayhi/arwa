Tomato Cucumber Salad





12 Minutes
90 Calories
9 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-1 tbsp lemon zest
-1/4 cup lemon juice
-1 tsp sugar
-2 tbsp low-fat mayonnaise
-1/4 cup low-fat sour cream
-1 tsp dill
-24 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
-6 sliced green onions, tops included
-3 cups peeled, diced cucumbers

Directions

  1. In a small bowl combine and mix the lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, mayonnaise, sour cream, and dill.
  2. In a large bowl combine the tomatoes, onions, and cucumbers.
  3. Add dressing to tomato mixture and mix well.
  4. Makes 4 servings.

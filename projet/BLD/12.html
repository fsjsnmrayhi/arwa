Three-bean Salad





15 Minutes
107 Calories
10 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-1 cup fresh green beans, cut into 1-inch lengths
-1 cup water
-1/3 (15-oz) can low-sodium garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
-1/3 (15-oz) can low-sodium kidney beans, drained and rinsed
-2 tbsp thinly sliced red onion
-1/4 cup sliced red bell pepper
-2 tsp olive oil
-1/4 cup red wine vinegar
-2 tsp sugar
-1 tsp salt-free Mrs. DashÂ® Lemon Pepper Seasoning Blend

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, bring the green beans and water to a boil.
  2. Cover. Reduce heat to simmer and cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until tender.
  3. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a large bowl combine the green beans, garbanzo beans, kidney beans, onion, and pepper.
  5. In a small bowl combine the olive oil, vinegar, sugar, and salt-free seasoning.
  6. Add the dressing to the bean mixture and mix well.
  7. Makes 4 servings.

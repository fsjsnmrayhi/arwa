Mushroom and Vegetable Scramble





15 Minutes
153 Calories
7 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-Nonstick cooking spray
-2 tsp olive oil
-8 green onions, diced
-1 cup diced red bell pepper
-4 cups sliced mushrooms
-2 cups egg substituter
-1/2 cup shredded, reduced-fat mild cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Spray a large skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Place the skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil.
  2. When hot, add onions, pepper, and mushrooms. Saut√© for 2 to 3 minutes or until mushrooms are tender.
  3. Add egg substitute and stir mixture continuously for 2 to 3 minutes or until cooked.
  4. Remove from heat and top with cheese.
  5. Makes 4 servings.

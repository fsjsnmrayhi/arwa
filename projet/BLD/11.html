Southern Black Bean Soup With Corn Muffins





50 Minutes
603 Calories
17 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-Recipe for Corn Muffins
-Nonstick cooking spray
-1 tsp canola oil
-11/2 cups chopped onion
-4 garlic cloves, minced
-1 1/2 cups chopped green bell pepper
-4 oz chopped smoked turkey
-1 cup chopped carrots
-1 (14 1/2-oz) can stewed, sliced tomatoes, no salt added
-1 (6-oz) can tomato paste, no salt added
-1 cup frozen, whole-kernel corn
-2 (15-oz) cans low-sodium black beans, drained
-1 tsp oregano
-1/2 tsp cumin
-1/4 tsp pepper
-1/4 tsp onion powder
-3 cups water

Directions

  1. Prepare Corn Muffins recipe according to directions.
  2. Spray a large saucepan with nonstick cooking spray. Place the saucepan over medium-high heat and add canola oil. When oil is hot, add onion, garlic, bell pepper, and smoked turkey. Saut√© for 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Add carrots, tomatoes, tomato paste, corn, black beans, oregano, cumin, pepper, onion powder, and water. Bring to a boil. Cover. Reduce heat to simmer and cook for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve with corn muffins.
  5. Makes 4 servings.

Smothered Okra and Tomatoes Over Rice





45 Minutes
435 Calories
13 Ingredients
4 Servings


Ingredients

-Nonstick cooking spray
-1 tbsp canola oil
-2 cups chopped onion
-4 garlic cloves, minced
-1 cup chopped green bell pepper
-2 cups frozen, whole-kernel corn
-1 (14 1/2-oz) can stewed, sliced tomatoes, no salt added
-6 oz chopped smoked turkey
-1/2 tsp thyme
-Dash cayenne pepper
-5 cups frozen, cut okra, thawed
-1/4 cup water
-1 cup brown rice, cooked according to directions without salt or fat

Directions

  1. Spray a large skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Place the skillet over medium-high heat and add canola oil. When oil is hot, add onion, garlic, and bell pepper. Saut√© for 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Add corn, tomatoes, smoked turkey, thyme, cayenne pepper, okra, and water. Bring to a boil. Cover. Reduce heat to simmer and cook 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Serve over hot brown rice
  4. Makes 4 servings.
